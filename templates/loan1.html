    Pradhan Mantri Kisan Credit Card (PMKCC)

    This scheme provides farmers with access to short-term credit for their agricultural needs. It simplifies the credit delivery system and offers interest rate concessions to farmers who repay loans on time.

    Go To Site

    Interest Subvention Scheme for Crop Loans

    Under this scheme, farmers are provided with short-term crop loans at a subsidized interest rate. If they repay the loan promptly, they can avail an additional interest rate reduction.




    Go To Site

    National Horticulture Mission (NHM)

    This is a crop insurance scheme that helps farmers mitigate financial losses due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases. The premium is heavily subsidized, making it affordable for farmers.


    Go To Site

    National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture

    While not specific to farmers, the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana offers loans to micro and small entrepreneurs, including those engaged in agriculture and allied activities.
